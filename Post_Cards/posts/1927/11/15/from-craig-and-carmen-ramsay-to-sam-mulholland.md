From: Craig and Carmen Ramsay, To: Sam Mulholland
Mailed From: Kansas on November 15, 1927

Mrs. Sam Mulholland
Pearl, Illinois
c/o Mrs. Chandler

Dear Mrs. Mulholland,
May I express my most sincere sympathy to you and your family in your bereavement. I know only too well what you are going through. I only wish I could do something to brighten your sorrow.
Sincerely,
Craig and Carmen Ramsay</body></html>
