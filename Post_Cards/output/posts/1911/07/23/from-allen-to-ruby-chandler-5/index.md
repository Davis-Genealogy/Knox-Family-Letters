From: Allen, To: Ruby Chandler
Mailed From: Normal, Illinois on July 23, 1911

Miss Ruby Chandler
Pearl, Illinois
(Crossed out: 503 N. School St. Normal,)

7-22-11
I am staying home every night so as to make up lots of lost sleep. Jim sends him regards.
Allen
