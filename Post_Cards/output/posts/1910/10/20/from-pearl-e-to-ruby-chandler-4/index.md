Pearl, Illinois
Pike Co.

Oct. 20 - 10
Dear Ruby,
Was sure glad to get your dandy letter. Will write soon as I can. Gee it's cold this a.m. I will have to build a fire today. Am getting along better now.
In haste,
Pearl</body></html>
