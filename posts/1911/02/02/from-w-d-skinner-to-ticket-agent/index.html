From: W.D. Skinner, To: Ticket Agent

Vincent Davis

1911-02-02 23:42

Source

From: W.D. Skinner, To: Ticket Agent Mailed From: Omaha, Nebraska on February 2, 1911 Postcard Info: A1 Published by Trattner Post Card Co., Seattle, Wash. Picture Info: Oregon & Washington Railroad Depot, Seattle, Wash. New North Pacific Coast Terminal Union pacific - Southern Pacific System.

Ticket Agent Chicago & Alton R.R., Pearl, Ill.

The name Oregon and Washington R.R., was on Dec., 24th, changed to Oregon-Washington Railroad and Navigation Co. The new Seattle station will be open for traffic about April 1, 1911 W.D. Skinner General Freight and Passenger Agent, Seattle, Wash.
