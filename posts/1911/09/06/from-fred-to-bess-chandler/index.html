From: Fred, To: Bess Chandler Postcard Info: M295-6. Printed in U.S.A. Picture Info: Copyright. 1910. by C. Hobson

Miss Bess Chandler Pearl, Ill

September 6 Dear Friend, I am coming through Pearl tomorrow. I suppose you are about it, down at school. Take good care of my ring and I will do the same ha ha. You had gone to school when I came up. Hope you don't whip anybody. Fred
