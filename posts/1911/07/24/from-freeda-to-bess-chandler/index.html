From: Freeda, To: Bess Chandler

Vincent Davis

Source

From: Freeda, To: Bess Chandler Picture Info: 1st M.E. Church, Milford, Illinois, Pub. by C.C.Campbell Mailed From: Milford, Illinois on July 24, 1911

Bessie Chandler Pearl, Illinois

Bess, Can I walk to school with you this a.m? Gee, I wish I could. Did you get home all O.K.? We 3 girls sure had a circus on our way home. Answer soon. Freeda
