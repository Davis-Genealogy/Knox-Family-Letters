From: Allen, To: Ruby Chandler

Vincent Davis

Source

From: Allen, To: Ruby Chandler Mailed From: Denver, Colorado on July 5, 1911 Picture Info: 7044. En Route to mt. McClellan, Colo., Taken June 18, 1909. Argentine Central Ry.

Miss Ruby Chandler 503 N. School St. Pearl, Illinois

7-5-11 Am sending you today a souvenir of Denver. Will leave here for Colorado Springs tomorrow. Enjoying myself immensely. Good Bye, Allen
