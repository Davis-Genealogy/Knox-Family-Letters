Source

From: Vivien Dawson, To: Bess Harleson Mailed From: Jacksonville, Illinois on August 18, 1912 Postcard Info: Pub. by A.H. Atherton, Jacksonville, Ill. Series 174. Picture Info:South Side, Public Square, Jacksonville, Ill.

Miss Bessie Harleson Louisiana, MO.

Please don't think I've forgotten you girlie, - it seems that way doesn't it? I often think of the people I met in Louisiana, so if you don't hear from me often, you'll still know I think of you! Say "hello" to Harvey for me. Vivien Dawson 120 N. Craivie Would love to hear from you.
