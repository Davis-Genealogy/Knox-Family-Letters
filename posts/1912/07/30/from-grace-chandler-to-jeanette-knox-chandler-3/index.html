From: Grace Chandler, To: Jeanette Knox Chandler

Vincent Davis

Source

From: Grace Chandler, To: Jeanette Knox Chandler Mailed From: Omaha, Nebraska on July 30, 1912 Picture Info: C. & N. W. Depot, Dunlap, Iowa.

Mrs. E.H. Chandler Pearl, Illinois

Omaha Dearest Mamma, Doesn't see possible that we are in the beautiful city of Omaha and have come across the state of Iowa. Sarchett is coming to Dunlap tomorrow. Grace
