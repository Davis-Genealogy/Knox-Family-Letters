From: Unknown, To: Knox Chandler Family

Vincent Davis

Source

From: Unknown, To: Knox Chandler Family Mailed From: Wichita, Kansas on September 10, 1912 Postcard Info: Published by C.A. Tanner & Co., Wichita Kansas. made in U.S.A. Picture Info: Missouri Pacific Depot, Wichita, Kansas

Mr. Knox Chandler Pearl, Illinois

Wichita 9/10 Dear Bro & all, Just came from Miller Business College and guess I will start next Monday. Am going to take short hand course. Can complete course in 6 months. How are ll of you? Write. Letter explaining course tonight.
