From: Eva Resterson, To: Bessie Chandler Printing Info: Bamforth & CO., New York

Miss Bessie Chandler Pearl, Ill

From Ma Dear Bessie, Just received your card and was glad to hear from Pa. Fredda and I are not very good friends. I was down to Virginia Saturday afternoon and wished for you. V.C said hello. Eva Resterson
