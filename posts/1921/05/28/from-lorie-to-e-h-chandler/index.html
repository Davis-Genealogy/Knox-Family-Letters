From: Lorie, To: Elijah H. Chandler Mailed From: Pana, Illinois on May 28, 1921

Mr. Elijah H. Chandler Pearl, Illinois

Pana, Illinois Dear Folks, We are going to try and make it and hop off tomorrow a.m. Will try and be there tomorrow night but do not know for sure. It may be Monday a.m. Love to all, Lorie Also, it is quite a city. Tell Grace and Ruby, if there, to go along.
