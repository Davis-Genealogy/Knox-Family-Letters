From: Pearl Ellis, To: Ruby Chandler Mailed From: Manchester, Illinois on October 20, 1910

Miss Ruby Chandler Pearl, Illinois Pike Co.

Oct. 20 - 10 Dear Ruby, Was sure glad to get your dandy letter. Will write soon as I can. Gee it's cold this a.m. I will have to build a fire today. Am getting along better now. In haste, Pearl
