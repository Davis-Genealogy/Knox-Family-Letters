From: Henry T. Rainey, To: Elijah H. Chandler Mailed From: Washington on December 18, 1915

E.H. Chandler Pearl, Ill

Greetings from Washington with best wishes for a Merry Christmas and a prosperous New Year, and may we not hope that our national prosperity may continue through the years to come and that we may remain at peace with all the world. Henry T. Rainey 1915
