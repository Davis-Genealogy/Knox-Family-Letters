From: Ruth, To: Ruby Chandler Postcard Info: Mailed from Mount Pulaski, Illinois on July 27, 1962 Picture Info: Court house in which Lincoln practiced law. Mt. Pulaski, Illinois 1962

Miss Ruby Chandler Pearl, Illinois Pike County

The pictures, I printed were all good-every one took that Sunday. Well on the good side, your mother and Bess can come up next week-well all are good and I wish to show her a good time. Hear you made a thourough study of chemistry . Well last beau-write Ruby!!! Write me , for oh so nightly------humor me. Soon ever, Ruth
